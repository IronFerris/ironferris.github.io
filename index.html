My dear friend,

I hope this letter finds you well. As you see it now before you, this is the town of █████████. All of my family heritage tracks back to this town, and yet none of my family ever bothered to mention it. Only upon researching our history through books stored away in our attic was I able to discover it.

To be honest, I no longer want to remain here. The residents trouble me, staring at me as if I am an outsider, which I suppose I am. They rarely have visitors come through, as their businesses have all run down and the factories that were once dispelling their waste have grown old, there is barely any reason for the people of █████████ to stay. The feeling of unbelonging in my chest does not grow from only the looks of the residents towards me, but with their looks as well. The people of █████████ have a strange look, with slightly bulging eyes and a wide frown on most faces. Everyone in this town has abnormaly large sizes for shoes, and their clothes are baggy and some even looks slightly wet. They stare at me, and I can feel their stares coming from the shadows...

But with this as the case, I would request your company. I am growing increasingly lonely in this place, reduced to staying in the cheapest hotel they have, which make strange noises at night. I have no neighbors next to my room, yet I somehow doubt that is the case. I know you will be on your way, so when you arrive, meet me at the shore. There is something I must show you.

Sincerely, Robert
